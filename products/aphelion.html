HONEST LIMITS

What Aphelion isn't

Live beta — small live sample

Aphelion went live in April 2026. The 6-year backtest covers a meaningful range of regimes, but past performance under fixed parameters is not a guarantee that the same parameters survive the next regime intact.

Not market-timing

The engine doesn't predict tops or bottoms. It buys oversold conditions and holds. Long stretches of grinding sideways action will see Aphelion accumulate slowly without exciting moves.

Long-bias by construction

2× isolated leverage on long positions only. In a generalized sell-off where every asset moves down together, Aphelion accumulates more aggressively. That's the design and the risk — it concentrates exposure to systemic moves.

Statistical, not deterministic

Drawdowns happen. Defense limits fill. Some positions trail-out at small losses. Do not commit capital you cannot afford to lose to a beta strategy.
