HOW IT WORKS

Zone-Defined Range Farming

Zone Aggregation — Every 30 minutes, the system fetches open interest and price data from Binance, Bybit, and OKX across selected assets. It computes liquidation max-pain zones — the price ranges where the highest concentration of leveraged positions sit. These zones create natural trading ranges: price tends to oscillate within them as liquidation cascades trigger snap-backs.

Bidirectional Entries — Unlike Zones (which only goes long into dips), Farms trades both directions. When price approaches the zone floor (long max pain), the bot opens longs. When price approaches the zone ceiling (short max pain), the bot opens shorts. Each trade targets a round-trip profit as price reverts to the zone middle.

Rotation & Risk — The bot continuously rotates across the highest-scoring assets based on zone width, volume, and open interest. Breakout stop-losses protect against range invalidation — if price escapes the zone, the position closes automatically.

RISK DISCLOSURE

Understanding the Risks

Soomario Farms trades leveraged perpetual futures in both directions on Hyperliquid. Bidirectional exposure means losses can accumulate on both sides during strong breakouts that invalidate the zone range.

Range-farming strategies perform best in sideways markets. Strongly trending markets can cause repeated stop-outs. Zone data is aggregated and estimated — zones can shift or disappear as market conditions change.
