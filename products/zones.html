HONEST LIMITS

What Zones isn't

Not a trend-follower

Zones is mean-reversion. It enters when prices have dislocated and exits when they reconverge. In sustained directional markets without liquidation cascades, the engine simply sits on its hands.

Edge depends on cascade frequency

In long quiet markets with low leverage and tight ranges, opportunities thin out and the engine produces less. The strategy works when there's something for it to mean-revert against.

Capacity-limited

The strategy operates on Hyperliquid order book depth. There's a real notional ceiling per coin before slippage degrades fills. Capacity scales with HL's liquidity, not infinitely.

Statistical, not deterministic

Drawdowns happen. Stops get hit. Some theses don't play out. The edge is statistical — past performance documents what the strategy has done, not what it will do.
