WHAT IT DOES

Smarter DCA, Not Blind DCA

Traditional dollar-cost averaging buys the same amount on a fixed schedule regardless of market conditions. The Accumulator is different — it only fires signals when assets are genuinely oversold, and it scales your buy amount based on signal strength.

Each signal is scored using a combination of RSI (relative strength) and price-versus-moving-average analysis. Stronger signals produce higher multipliers, meaning you buy more when conditions are most favorable and less (or nothing) when they aren't.

You set a monthly budget. The system calculates a safe daily allocation across all 14 assets. When a signal fires, it tells you exactly how much to buy — not just "buy" but "$23.45 of NVDA today." Signals are delivered instantly via Telegram.

HOW IT WORKS

Signal to Buy in 4 Steps

Scan

The system monitors all 14 assets using RSI and price-versus-moving-average indicators on a daily timeframe.

Score

When an asset crosses the oversold threshold, a signal strength multiplier (1x–5x) is calculated based on how deeply oversold it is.

Size

Your monthly budget is divided into a safe daily allocation. The multiplier scales the buy amount: a 3x signal on a $10/day rate = "$30 of NVDA today."

Signal

A Telegram message arrives instantly with asset, buy amount, signal strength, and price. You execute on your own brokerage.
