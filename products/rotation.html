HONEST LIMITS

What Rotation isn't

Paper trading — not live capital

Live performance will differ materially due to slippage, funding costs, gap risk, and operational execution friction. Transition to live deployment is contingent on 90+ trading days of continuous operation and validation against expected ranges.

Correlation in coordinated selloffs

Stocks, crypto longs, and crypto shorts all benefit from a general risk-on environment. In a coordinated selloff, only the short sleeve provides genuine diversification — and it's capped at 10% of capital.

Single-venue execution

Hyperliquid is the sole execution venue. A prolonged Hyperliquid outage would freeze all strategy activity. Stock perpetuals are only available through Hyperliquid's stock-perp DEX, so there's no fallback for the equity sleeve.

Statistical, not deterministic

Past paper-trading performance doesn't guarantee future results. The sample is small. The strategy depends on the assumption that visionary equity targets remain credible and that crypto liquidation cascades happen with reasonable frequency.
