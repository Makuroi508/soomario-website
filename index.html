QUESTIONS

FAQ

Can I start with one service and upgrade later?

Yes! Many clients start with Accumulator ($19.99/mo) or Max Pain ($39.99/mo), then upgrade to Elite when ready for full automation. Your Accumulator and Max Pain signals are included in Elite, so you won't lose anything.

Do I need to move my funds to Soomario?

No. All strategies operate with self-custody. For signals, you execute on your own exchange. For Elite, you connect your own Hyperliquid account via API. For the Max Pain vault, your funds remain on-chain and withdrawable at any time.

Which service has the highest returns?

Elite's three bots show backtested returns of 407% (HYPE), 2,211% (AVAX), and 973% (SOL) with an average 78.6% win rate. Max Pain backtested 4,807% in Year 1 — with important caveats about compounding at scale explained on the Max Pain page. Accumulator backtested 597% ROI on deployed capital across 14 assets.

What's the minimum capital needed?

Accumulator works with any amount — start with $500 and add monthly. Max Pain and Elite work best with $1,000+ for proper position sizing across all strategies. $5,000+ is optimal for Elite.

Can I lose money?

Yes. All trading involves risk of loss. Our strategies use stop losses, trailing stops, and circuit breakers to limit drawdowns, but losses are an inherent part of trading. Leveraged perpetual futures carry additional risk including potential liquidation. Never trade with funds you cannot afford to lose. We show both wins and losses — transparency is our core value.
