No. All strategies operate with self-custody. For signals, you execute on your own exchange. For Elite and Max Pain, you can subscribe monthly or deposit into a Hyperliquid vault — your funds remain on-chain and withdrawable at any time.

Accumulator works with any amount — start with $500 and add monthly. Max Pain and Elite work best with $1,000+. $5,000+ is optimal for Elite.

Yes. All trading involves risk of loss. Our strategies use stop losses, trailing stops, and circuit breakers, but losses are inherent. Leveraged perps carry additional liquidation risk. Never trade with funds you cannot afford to lose.
