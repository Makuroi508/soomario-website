Understanding the Risks

The Contrarian bot trades perpetual futures with 5x leverage on OKX. Leverage amplifies both gains and losses. A 6% adverse move triggers the stop loss, equating to a 30% loss on position margin.

Contrarian strategies can underperform during prolonged directional markets. The bot is designed for mean-reversion — it will miss strong trending moves by design.

Live testing results represent a short time period. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Never invest more than you can afford to lose.
