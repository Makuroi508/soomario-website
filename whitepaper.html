SECTION II

The Soomario Philosophy

2.1 Why Three Services?

We designed three distinct services instead of one because different traders have different needs:

Accumulator provides opportunities across stocks and major cryptocurrencies for those who want to execute trades manually while following proven strategies.

Elite is the all-inclusive premium package for clients who want everything - copy trading for the flagship strategy plus access to all signal services.

Max Pain offers choice - follow signals manually or let OKX copy trading handle everything automatically, with 20 strategies for maximum diversification.

2.3 Risk Management Principles

Position Sizing

Every strategy uses dynamic position sizing based on available capital. No single position can exceed predetermined limits, protecting against catastrophic losses.

Leverage Matching

We match leverage to proven risk profiles. Low drawdown strategies get higher leverage (up to 8x). High drawdown strategies get conservative leverage (3x).

Circuit Breakers

Automated systems pause new entries when margin ratios exceed thresholds to prevent cascade liquidations during extreme volatility.

Diversification

Elite uses 5 strategies across 5 different assets. Accumulator covers 14 assets with 0.05 average correlation. Max Pain employs 20 strategies.
